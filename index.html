02 — The Studio

Two people.
One obsession.

Most brands don't fail because the product is bad. They fail because nobody could see what made it good.

You don't have a product problem. You just haven't used what makes your brand tick yet. Whether you can't figure out how to make a website or you're already doing volume that just isn't converting — we can help.

Cold Press is Mitchell Corkery and Josh Ongley. One person who understands branding down to the bone. One who can build anything from scratch. We started this because real conviction deserves better than a template and a retainer. Between us there's no question we can't answer and no brief we can't diagnose. You get a straight answer every time — not the one that keeps the invoice going.

We want to find the next Patagonia before they can afford a proper launch. The first call a founder makes when they have a genuinely good idea — whether there's $200k behind it or they're still working the job. The kind of people still asking about your numbers four months later. Not because it's in the contract — because the day you can quit your job, we want to be the ones taking you to dinner.

That's not copy. That's conviction. That's actually the goal.

Before we quote you anything, we'll tell you exactly what we think your brand needs. Whether you hire us or not.
