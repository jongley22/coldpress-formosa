Our Story

Sixteen years.
Still making it here.

Formosa Foods was built in Christchurch by a family who understood that great food starts with craft, not shortcuts. For sixteen years we have been making noodles, pastries, and dumplings the right way — from a kitchen in Burnside, for tables all over New Zealand.

Family owned. Proudly Christchurch. We supply Chinese restaurants, golf clubs, and food service venues across the South Island — and now, through Bidfood, the rest of New Zealand.
